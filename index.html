Frozen Gears

A winter-themed Create modpack for Minecraft

About Frozen Gears

Frozen Gears is a winter wonderland of mechanical creativity built around the Create mod. This carefully balanced modpack transforms Minecraft into a snow-covered industrial paradise where engineering and magic blend seamlessly.

Explore frigid landscapes, build magnificent machines, and automate your way through the eternal winter. Every mod has been selected to enhance the snowy theme while maintaining excellent performance.

Key Features

Create Mechanics

Harness the power of Create's mechanical systems to build intricate contraptions in a winter setting.

Winter Wonderland

Explore beautiful snow-covered biomes and ice formations with enhanced winter atmosphere.

Performance Optimized

Carefully selected mods that improve FPS while delivering a rich winter experience.
